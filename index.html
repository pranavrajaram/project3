Where's My Water? How Different Body Parts React to Dehydration

Drag the slider and hover over body parts to see how running on a treadmill impacts the body!

Visualization Context:

  • Participants ran on a treadmill for 120 minutes without fluid intake, divided into 8 running intervals (15 min each).
  • Data was aggregated across each time interval, and the averages for each body part are shown.
  • What is Ohm? Ohm (Œ©) is a unit of electrical resistance. In bioimpedance measurements, higher Ohm values indicate greater resistance, meaning lower water content, while lower Ohm values suggest higher hydration levels.
